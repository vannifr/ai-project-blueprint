### The Cost Overview (TCO)

*What are the total costs for development and management?*

- Initial data acquisition or tooling.
- **Usage Costs (Opex):**
- API / Token costs per month.
- Compute / Hosting (Cloud).
- Maintenance & Monitoring by team.
